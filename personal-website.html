I am a researcher in urban anthropology, former affiliate to Leicester University's Department of Geography and to Harvard University's Department of Anthropology, currently a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Roma Tre, and a member of the Catalan Institute of Anthropology. I conducted ethnographic research in Rome, Barcelona, Casablanca, Managua, and consultancy activities for NGOs and international public agencies. I organized Harvard's 1st Urban activism conference, and was an invited speaker at the 2023 Boston University Initiative on Cities Gentrification and Displacement conference.

I am interested to the social and cultural impact of development induced displacement and gentrification. I campaign for the right to housing for all, and defend squatting and self-built neighborhoods as guarantees of the social function of private property. A just distribution of housing and land is crucial to face climatic breakdown and the social transformations associated, and anthropology is an important tool to proceed in this direction. I am interested in ritual and folk religion as devices to maintain relations inside communities and with the environment in times of crises.

I have a MA in Cultural Anthropology (vecchio ordinamento) and a PhD in Urban Studies at Sapienza University of Rome. In 2017-2020 I was granted a EU-funded Marie Curie Global Fellowship, and in 2022 I reached the 2nd step of evaluation for an ERC Starting Grant project, to be developed at Ca' Foscari University of Venice. My mentors are Professor Loretta Lees, Professor Michael Herzfeld, Professor Manuel Delgado. I also grew up with an oral historian.

I am especially fond of the Moroccan Gnawa cultural complex, and I support Casablanca's association Gnaoua Culture. I founded Barcelona's Periferies Urbanes research group and website, and I am a member of University of Barcelona's OACU (Observatory of Anthropology of Urban Conflict) and GRECS (Grup de Recerca en Exclusió i Control Social). I always write on the Italian websites Monitor, Napoli Monitor, and on the independent journal Lo stato delle città.

I campaign for the right to move and for the right to stay put; I support self-determination and liberation from exploitation, colonization and neocolonization for all people on earth, including the Kurdish and the Palestinian people, undergoing decades of violent occupation, apartheid and violation of basic rights, in breach of international treaties and UN resolutions, for the sake of corporate profit and political supremacy. Israel's genocide of the Palestinian people exposed the misery of an international order based on white suprematism and lies, which is our duty to dismantle, from the bottom up, with all means necessary.
